tcl_youtube_dontstop.tcl

#scope
This program is able to keep youtube running for ever wihout being stop.
Every 5 seconds there will be a Screenshot of the desktop.
This image is cropped to the exact location of the message box.
By extracting the letters in the image, a decision is made to fire a <left mouse click>.
Deleting the image is happenning in each case.

1. Print screen each 5sec
2. Exctracting letters
3. Fire <left mouse click>
